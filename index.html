Matthew E. Weingarten

I'm an incoming PhD student at Columbia University, interested in making efficient use of computing hardware with profile guided compiler optimizations, operating systems, and computer architecture. I have worked with ARM CoreSight on the Enzian platform in the ETH Zurich systems group, including building a high-throughput decoder in hardware, and developed optimizations for GraalVM compiler at Oracle Labs.
